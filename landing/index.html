Built for how you actually spend

From AI parsing to budget alerts — ExpenseBeam handles the full picture of your personal finances.

🤖

AI Expense Parsing

Type naturally or paste WhatsApp messages. Claude AI extracts every expense with the correct amount, date, and category — instantly.

📊

Budget Management

Set monthly budgets per category. Get amber warnings at 80% and red alerts at 100% — always visible on your dashboard.

🔮

Monthly AI Insights

Each month, AI analyzes your spending patterns and delivers a personalized insight — what changed, where you overspent, and how to improve.

💱

Multi-currency

Record expenses in any currency. Set exchange rates manually. All reports normalize to your home currency for consistent totals.

📥

Smart CSV Import

Drag-and-drop any CSV file. AI auto-maps your columns to the right fields, shows a preview, and imports cleanly — no reformatting needed.

📈

Reports & Charts

Spending trends, category breakdowns, month-over-month comparisons, and top spending days — all in beautiful interactive charts.

🔄

Recurring Expenses

Set up rent, subscriptions, and regular bills once. ExpenseBeam auto-generates the expense on the due date — zero manual effort.

🔍

Global Search

Press Ctrl+K anywhere to instantly search across all expenses by description, tag, or note.

🌙

Dark Mode

A beautiful dark interface that's easy on the eyes. Your preference syncs to your account — consistent across every session.
